Momos Recipe


Ingredients:

  • Carrots and cabbage
  • soya sauce
  • vinegar
  • black pepper
  • 2 cups meida
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps to make momos

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt. Gradually add warm water and knead to form a soft dough. Cover with a damp cloth and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  2. In a pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add the ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for a minute.
  3. Add the chopped onions and cook until translucent. Then add the cabbage and carrots. Cook for another 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in the soy sauce, and season with salt and pepper. Cook for a few more minutes until the vegetables are tender. Remove from heat and let the filling cool.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls and roll each ball into a thin circle on a floured surface.
  6. Spoon a small amount of filling into the center of each dough circle. Fold the edges over to seal the momo, pinching and pleating to ensure it is tightly closed.
  7. Steam the momos in a steamer for 10-15 minutes, or until the dough is cooked through and translucent.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
