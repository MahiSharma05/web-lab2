The Power of Habit

At its core, The Power of Habit contains an exhilarating argument: The key to exercising regularly, losing weight, raising exceptional children, becoming more productive, building revolutionary companies and social movements, and achieving success is understanding how habits work. Habits aren't destiny.The book uncovers the psychology and neuroscience behind our daily routines. It presents practical strategies for replacing bad habits with positive ones. Real-life examples and case studies make the content relatable and engaging.Reading this book you will be astounded at how much your daily life is governed by habit: not by decisive actions, but by habit. (Just merely observing the habits involved in brushing my teeth was an amazing revelation of the number of habits instilled into a day.)
